### Why 01.AI, Why Now (March 2023)

**The ChatGPT Catalyst**:
The breakthrough success of ChatGPT in early 2023 crystallized Lee's conviction that foundation models represented "a historic opportunity that China cannot miss." Lee observed that "by some measures, we've already achieved AGI" with modern LLMs, making the timing critical.

**Market Necessity**:
Lee articulated a crucial insight: "Unlike the rest of the world, China doesn't have access to OpenAI and Google because those two companies did not make their products available in China. There's clearly a huge necessity in China. Many developers are trying to create a solution for a market that really needs this."

**The Decision to Act**:
Lee emphasized this was "the most important thing he's done," reflecting deep conviction. He applied Steve Jobs' philosophy: "Life cannot be planned endlessly for the future, but if you look back, everything you've done can connect into a line. If each decision follows your heart, they will eventually prove correct."

**Unique Position**:
At 60+ years old, Lee could have remained as Sinovation Ventures chairman. Instead, he chose operational leadership at 01.AI, reflecting the magnitude of the opportunity. Notably, he did NOT leave Sinovation Ventures‚Äîmaintaining his chairman role while serving as CEO of 01.AI, believing the LLM opportunity required direct involvement rather than just investment.

### Strategic Pivots and Decisions

**From Scaling to Efficiency**:
Early emphasis on scaling large foundation models shifted to building cost-effective, specialized models. Yi-Lightning's extreme cost-efficiency (0.14x GPT-4 launch price) became the differentiation strategy.

**Open Source Embrace**:
In mid-2024, 01.AI made a significant strategic decision to bet heavily on open-source models (DeepSeek partnership) rather than competing directly with proprietary closed models. This reflected recognition that DeepSeek's technical achievements dominated the open-source landscape.

**Market Necessity Focus**:
Lee emphasized the core market gap: "Unlike the rest of the world, China doesn't have access to OpenAI and Google...many are trying to create a solution for a market that really needs this." This remained the North Star even as product strategy evolved.

**Profitability as Existential**:
By 2024, Lee was direct about the challenge: "Too many LLM startups focus on breakthrough advances rather than commercialization...this is about survival." 01.AI's pivot to revenue-generating products reflected this urgency.
