# NYSE Overview: Number of Stocks and Top 10 by Market Cap

### Number of Stocks Listed on the NYSE:

The number of companies or stocks listed on the New York Stock Exchange (NYSE) shows some slight variation across sources due to factors like the exact counting methodology (e.g., companies vs. distinct stock classes) and the date of the data.

*   Generally, there are **over 2,300 companies** listed on the NYSE. Some sources indicate this number can be closer to 2,400 companies.
*   When counting distinct stock listings, the number can be around **1,900 to 2,000 total stocks**.
